One of the clearest signs the model works is when student work outgrows the
campus it started on. A collaboration between Carleton and UCLA students produced
a Tableau visualization that ended up presented at conferences on both sides of
the Atlantic.

The point isn't the tool. It's that an undergraduate analyst took a real research
question, built something rigorous enough to stand in front of a professional
audience, and got the experience of defending it. That's the outcome the network
is built to produce, and to make visible.
